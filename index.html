A Little About Me

I was born in Knoxville, TN on November 22, 1988. I am the youngest of three children, my siblings both being sisters. They are both named Allison which is an intersting coincidence (we refer to them by their respective middle initials). My oldest sister is an Anesthesiologist and the other one is a Lawyer. Both practice in Knoxville. I've lived with my parents in middle Tennessee since 1994, in the Brentwood/Franklin area.

Some hobbies I enjoy are watching college football, playing wheelchair sports, pretending I know what is going on in politics, and playing around with my dog.
